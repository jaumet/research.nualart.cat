CAREX-CAT is a system of information on occupational exposure to carcinogens in Catalonia. Idea(Concept & code is developed by Jaume Nualart. The data is open and collected by the by the Catalan Institute for Occupational Health and Safety (ICSSL) of the Department of Labor, Social Affairs and Families of the Generalitat (gov.) of Cataloniaa.
This system follows the criteria of the European Carcinogen Exposure project and provides an estimatiob of the working population exposed by carcinogenic agent and economic activity.
