Welcome to the Alluvial of Mentions (AoM) in Peace Agreements database (PA-X).

AoM is a tracker of mentions of concepts along the PA-X collection of peace agreements.

What is about:

This site presents Alluvial diagrams (in most cases a synonym of Sankey diagram). Each diagram presents from left to right these columns: Category names > Issue names > sub-issue names. Each of the 293 names has a vertical width relative to times that name is mentioned in the Peace Agreements collection (version 3).
