ICML 2013 Simple Topic Model

Background

ICML 2013 PDF papers are downloaded from the JMLR site. A simple text processor hueristically converts each paper into multiple sections without header information, acknowledgements or references. Each section is then given to the topic modelling algorithm as a "document" using a very strict tokenisation scheme to delete the weird mathematical symbols rife in ICML papers.

For each topic we give:
  • proposed name,
  • percent proportion as p
  • word sparsity as ws, i.e., 80% means only 20% of vocabulary occures in topic,
  • document sparsity as ds, i.e., 80% means only 20% of documents have some fraction of this,
  • most prevalent papers/sections with this topic, title and link to abstyract at JMLR.
